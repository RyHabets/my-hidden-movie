What movie and what is it about?

My favorite movie is The Crow (2024) a dark supernatural revenge tale centered on Eric (Bill Skarsgård), a recovering addict who finds love with musician Shelly (FKA twigs). Their bond is brutally shattered when both are murdered by a crime syndicate tied to Shelly her past. Eric is resurrected by a mysterious spirit named Kronos, returning to the living world with otherworldly powers to hunt those responsible and save the shoul of Shelly.

As Eric takes on the syndicate, he uncovers painful secrets and battles his own mortality, dying multiple times before striking a final deal with the afterlife. In a climactic showdown, Eric sacrifices himself to defeat the villains and give Shelly a second chance at life. Though separated by death, their souls remain connected, hinting at a love that transcends even the grave.

MIXED OPINIONS

The Crow (2024) received mixed reactions from both critics and viewers. Many reviewers said the story was hard to follow and the film lacked emotion. However, some praised its dark style and visual design. Outlets like CGMagazine pointed out that fans of the original comic or actor Bill Skarsgård might still enjoy it.
